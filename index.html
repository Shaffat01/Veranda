Welcome to Veranda

A welcoming space for every occasion

Veranda is Bensalem's modern, flexible event venue designed for celebrations of all kinds. From weddings and showers to birthdays, cultural events, and corporate gatherings, our inviting space adapts effortlessly to your vision.

With a private entrance, elegant ballroom, outdoor terrace with firepits, and the convenience of Spark by Hilton Bensalem just steps away, Veranda makes hosting simple, seamless, and memorable.

Your moments matter here, and we're honored to help you celebrate them.
